Fury's Story

Look, I'm no professional.

I never have, might never be, but what i'm doing here might help. My reasoning is that i've tried everything there is to offer in the MC community, but I have yet to master anything. I'm getting close with my Render series, but it's no Weedlion. I have tried audio design (ended up with some spectacular fails), Youtube LP's (ended with people ripping me off while "including" me in their SMP), art design (stick figures man, they aren't the best.), building (detail shouldn't be that small, it's Minecraft for crying out loud), Resource packs (Well, more of a custom music pack), and my latest attempt: Graphic Design. I make my own thread designs, logos, profile pictures, and textures. I'm no savant, but I have some sense of palatte. As you can see, it's not horrid.
